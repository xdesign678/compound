Features

Everything a serious writer needs

Inline suggestions

Get contextual rewrites and improvements directly inside your document — accept or discard with a single keystroke.

Voice & tone memory

Quill learns how you write. Paste a few samples and it matches your vocabulary, cadence, and personal style with precision.

Smart outlines

Describe your idea in a sentence and Quill builds a structured outline. Rearrange, expand, or collapse sections as you think.

Real-time feedback

Clarity scores, readability grades, and grammar notes surface as you type — no need to run a separate check.

Format flexibility

Export to Markdown, HTML, plain text, or Notion. Quill preserves every heading, list, and footnote through conversion.

Privacy first

Your drafts are never used for training. End-to-end encryption means your words stay yours — always.
